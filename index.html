Study Tips

  • Create a consistent study schedule and stick to it.
  • Find a quiet and comfortable place to study.
  • Use techniques like the Pomodoro method (25 min study, 5 min break).
  • Review your notes regularly to reinforce learning.
  • Explain concepts to a friend to check your understanding.
